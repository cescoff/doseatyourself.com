About

Marseille, June 11, 2018

We all are wasting : it is our way of life and we cannot avoid it. But this is a “too much” way of life. We, all, know it. At least, I hope, some of us are aware of it.

This page is an invitation to dive into the waste, dive into what we do no longer want, what we forget, lose, let, hate and so on… It is an invitation to discover the “soul” of our waste, the beauty of it and its tremendous potential.

This page is a collection of pictures, solutions, results, works, videos, tutorials that shows the state of our life style output and ways to reuse it.

Here, you won't feel guilty of your wasting prompention. You won't see your impact on the planet, but, you will fall in love with your waste and discover the value of it.

Let’s dive into the waste and its wild beauty, let’s change our point of view, let’s focus on the rubbish.

Of course it is actually utopia: that is the beauty of it
